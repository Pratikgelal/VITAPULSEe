What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate — calories your body needs at complete rest for vital functions like breathing and circulation.

What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure — your BMR multiplied by activity level. This is the total calories you burn each day.

Setting Your Goal

Eat 300–500 kcal below TDEE to lose weight, at TDEE to maintain, or above to gain muscle mass.
